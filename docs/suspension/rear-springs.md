## Sedan (solid axle)

!!! info "Key Information"
    - Free height: 415mm
    - Top OD: 134mm
    - Top ID: 108mm
    - Bottom OD: 109mm
    - Bottom ID: 85mm
    - _Source: [Clint Castellano](https://www.facebook.com/notes/x3-chassis-cressida-club/x3x4-cressidamark-iichaser-faq/677056622503058/)_
